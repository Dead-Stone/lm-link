# Privacy Policy — LM Link for Android

**Last updated:** June 9, 2026  
**App:** LM Link for Android (`com.lmlink.android`)  
**Contact:** [mohanmoganti2023@gmail.com](mailto:mohanmoganti2023@gmail.com)

## Summary

LM Link is a mobile client for [LM Studio](https://lmstudio.ai). The app runs on your Android device and connects to **your** computers and model files. We do not operate a cloud backend for chat, and we do not sell your data.

## What LM Link does

- Connects to an LM Studio server you configure (typically on your home Wi‑Fi).
- Stores conversations and settings **on your device**.
- Can download GGUF model files you choose (from curated links or URLs you paste) and run them locally on your phone.
- Can attach photos you select for vision-capable models.

## Information stored on your device

LM Link may store the following locally on your phone or tablet:

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Chat messages and conversation titles | Conversation history |
| Server URLs and connection profiles | Reconnect to your LM Studio instance |
| API tokens (if you add one) | Authenticate with your LM Studio server when required |
| App preferences (theme, defaults, prompts) | Settings |
| Downloaded model files (GGUF) | On-device inference |
| Image attachments you add to chats | Sent to your chosen model when you send a message |

Sensitive values such as API tokens may be stored using the platform secure storage APIs where available.

## Information sent over the network

When you use LM Link, network traffic is between **your device** and **endpoints you configure**, for example:

- Your LM Studio server on your local network (`http://192.168.x.x:1234/v1` or similar).
- Hugging Face or other URLs **you** choose when downloading a model.
- Public model catalog/search endpoints used to browse the model library.

**We do not receive your chats, prompts, or model outputs.** LM Link does not include analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs that send data to the app developer.

## Permissions (Android)

LM Link may request:

| Permission | Why |
|------------|-----|
| Internet / network state | Connect to LM Studio and download models |
| Wi‑Fi / multicast | Discover LM Studio on your local network |
| Camera | Take photos to attach in chat (optional) |
| Read media (images/video) | Pick photos from your gallery for chat (optional) |

You can deny camera or media access and still use text chat and remote models.

## Data retention and deletion

- Conversations and settings remain on your device until you delete them in the app or use **Settings → Clear All Data**.
- Uninstalling the app removes app data from your device subject to Android behavior.
- Downloaded on-device model files are stored in the app’s private storage and removed when you delete them in the Model Library or clear app data.

## Children’s privacy

LM Link is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Third-party services

- **LM Studio** — software you run on your own computer; governed by LM Studio’s terms and policies.
- **Hugging Face** — when you download models from Hugging Face URLs, their terms and privacy policy apply to that download.
- **Google Play** — if you install from the Play Store, Google’s policies apply to the store transaction and device services.
